Valorant:

Valorant is a free-to-play tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It was officially released on June 2, 2020 for Microsoft Windows. The game combines traditional elements of shooters like Counter-Strike with hero-based abilities similar to Overwatch.

Players are divided into two teams of five, with one side attacking and the other defending. The main mode involves planting or defusing a bomb-like device called the Spike. Matches are played in multiple rounds, and the first team to win 13 rounds is the winner.

Each player selects a character known as an Agent, each with unique abilities that add strategic depth to gunfights. Despite the powers, accurate shooting and economy management remain core to success.

When you start an account, Valorant gives to you 5 agents (characters):

  • Jett
  • Phoenix
  • Sova
  • Brimstone
  • Sage
