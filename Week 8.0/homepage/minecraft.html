MINECRAFT

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game created by Markus "Notch" Persson and later developed and published by Mojang Studios. The game was first released in 2009 and officially launched in 2011. It allows players to explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world, where they can gather resources, craft tools, build structures, and survive against enemies like zombies and skeletons.

There are different modes to play:

  • Survival Mode, where players must manage health and hunger while collecting resources.
  • Creative Mode, which gives unlimited resources and the freedom to build without restrictions.
  • Adventure Mode, designed for custom maps and challenges.
  • Spectator Mode, where players can freely explore without interacting with the world.

Minecraft Legends

Minecraft Legends is an action-strategy video game developed by Mojang Studios and Blackbird Interactive, and published by Xbox Game Studios. It was released on April 18, 2023. Unlike the original Minecraft, this game focuses on real-time strategy and combat rather than building and crafting.

The story is set in the Minecraft universe, where players must unite villagers, animals, and familiar creatures like Creepers and Zombies to defend the Overworld from an invasion of Piglins coming from the Nether.

Gameplay combines exploration, resource gathering, and commanding armies. Players ride a mount, lead allies into battle, and build defenses in real-time. It also features both single-player and multiplayer modes, allowing cooperative play or competitive matches against other players.

Even though it shares the Minecraft style and characters, it is a very different experience—more about strategy and battles than open-world creativity.

The game keeps Minecraft’s blocky style but adds cinematic battles and new animations. Players explore biomes, team up with mobs, and use strategy to fight the Piglins, creating a fresh story within the Minecraft universe.

Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons is an action-adventure dungeon crawler game developed by Mojang Studios and Double Eleven, released in 2020. Unlike the original Minecraft, this game does not focus on building or mining. Instead, it is inspired by classic dungeon crawlers, where players explore levels filled with enemies, traps, and treasures.

The story follows the player’s fight against the Arch-Illager, a villain who uses the Orb of Dominance to control monsters and spread chaos. Players battle through different locations, defeat powerful bosses, and collect weapons, armor, and artifacts to grow stronger.

The game supports single-player and multiplayer (online and local co-op), allowing up to four players to fight together. With its simple controls, loot system, and blocky Minecraft art style, Minecraft Dungeons offers a fast-paced and fun experience while keeping the charm of the Minecraft universe.
